Section 01

About Me

Hello! I’m Md Tasneemul Hassan, a final-year Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) undergraduate student at BRAC University.

My academic journey is driven by a deep fascination with the intersection of hardware and intelligence. While my foundation is rooted in core electrical engineering ranging from Electronic Circuits and VLSI Design to Control Systems and Embedded Systems, I have dedicated the latter part of my degree to exploring the frontier of modern technology Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Neural Networks, and Robotics.

Throughout my time at BRAC University, I have sought to bridge the gap between theoretical and practical, real world application. Currently, I am channeling this knowledge into my Research Papers and Final Year Design Project (Capstone), focusing on building smarter, more efficient systems.

Beyond the classroom and lab, I am an active contributor to the campus tech community. As I am President of the BRAC University Electrical and Electronic Club (BUEEC) , I collaborate with fellow innovators, organize tech events, and constantly push the boundaries of what we can create.

Whether it’s optimizing an embedded system, creating, or designing a complex circuit, I thrive on solving difficult engineering problems. As I approach graduation, I am eager to transition my skills into industry roles or research opportunities where I can help build the intelligent systems of tomorrow.
