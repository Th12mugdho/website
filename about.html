Trained on circuits,
built for systems.

I graduated from BRAC University with a BSc in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, having spent my final year leading BUEEC as President, and building Payra — a hexacopter-based autonomous non-landing drone delivery system for urban food and medicine delivery in Dhaka.

My work sits at the intersection of embedded control, simulation, and applied machine learning — from PX4 flight-stack tuning to bio-inspired neural network design.
